# ⚖️ LEGAL TRUTH & COMPLIANCE (MP-LEGAL-01)

## 1. Age-Gate Integrity
- **Logic**: No user can view the "Boutique" without completing the **Outworld Age-Verification**.
- **Requirement**: Use a "Self-Certification" modal that logs the user's IP and timestamp to `.context/legal/compliance_log.json`.

## 2. Documentation Linkage
- **Smart-Link**: The "About Us" page must contain a "Transparency Vault" where users can view our legal licensing and Lesotho certification via a secure PDF viewer.
- **Physical Truth**: Every product in the R180 Reserve tier must link to its specific **Batch Lab Result** (PDF) stored in the repo.

## 3. Data Privacy (Lesotho Standards)
- **Encryption**: All "Inner Circle" user data must be hashed before being sent to the database.
- **Protocol**: Maloti Prime does not store plaintext passwords. We only store "Access Hashes" to protect the Kingdom's users.
